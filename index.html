IoT Lab

  • Who we are
    Welcome to the website of the Internet of Things Lab (IoT Lab) within the Computer Engineering and Informatics Department at the University of Patras, Greece. The Lab is led by Professor Sotiris Nikoletseas. Our Lab is dedicated to advancing the field through cutting-edge research in the design, analysis, and implementation of highly efficient algorithms, systems, applications, and testbeds for Wireless Sensor Networks and the Internet of Things, as well as their underlying foundations and principles.
  • Balancing Academia and Industry
    Our work is grounded in both academic exploration and real-world implementation. We believe in creating effective solutions that are not only theoretically sound but also applicable and useful in various industries.

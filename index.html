IoT Lab

  • Who we are
    • Welcome to the official website of the Internet of Things Lab (IoT Lab) within the Computer Engineering and Informatics Department at the University of Patras, Greece. The lab is under the leadership of Professor Sotiris Nikoletseas. Our lab is dedicated to advancing the field through cutting-edge research in the development, analysis, and implementation of highly efficient algorithms, systems, applications, and testbeds for Wireless Sensor Networks and the Internet of Things.

  • Balancing Academia and Industry
    • Our work is grounded in both academic exploration and real-world implementation. We believe in creating solutions that are not only theoretically sound but also applicable and useful in various industries.
